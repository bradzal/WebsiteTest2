About ProVantage Automation

Design - Integration - Support

ProVantage Automation provides automation controls design and integration services for automated manufacturing in southern Ontario. We are a controls system designer, integrator and support group for automated machine, line and process control. We are industry experts in PLC programming, motion control & robotics, HMIs, and supervisory control systems. ProVantage offers complete, turn-key automation controls packages that include design, testing, installation, training, start-up support, and 24/7 on-call support. What sets us apart from OEMs and other Automation systems integrators is our commitment to a complete solution. We follow the project right through to completion, and guarantee we will be there when you need us. ProVantage's core three partners have a combined experience of over 50 years creating advanced, reliable automation solutions. Our main area of expertise lies within End of Line Packaging, Paper Converting and Material Handling, but we pride ourselves on keeping up to date with the latest technology and possess a strong foundation in all fields of industrial automation. We support all major brands of controls hardware/software and we proudly stand behind all of our work.
