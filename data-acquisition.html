Supervisory Control & Data Acquisition

image slider by WOWSlider.com v5.5
ProVantage offers control options for our customers, giving them access to their operations from a supervisory perspective. We implement and provide controls that monitor, record and produce reports that outline performance, highlight concerns, and provide the reports you need to ensure you are meeting the maximum efficiency potential of your operations.

Asset Optimization
Using advanced diagnostics and our deep experience, we can often find places where your manufacturing line is not operating at maximum efficiency. Subtle improvements in the interactions between workers, components and machines themselves can make large difference to the big picture.

Line Harmonization
Eliminate backlogs and jams by having your manufacturing line synchronized and communicating throughout each station. We offer supervisory control systems that can give you peace of mind and significantly reduce the downtime caused by unintelligent controls.

Data Acquisiton & Reporting
ProVantage takes supervisory control a step further by recording the values that are important to your process and providing a Historian database to store the information in so that reports using various collected values can be compared. Data acquisition and analysis plays a large role in highlighting areas of poor performance and improvement focus that are not initially easily identified.

Individual & Full Process Solutions
ProVantage utilizes two solutions to accomplish this; the eWON Flexy & Invensys' Wonderware. The eWON device provides entry level data-logging, alarming, and remote access troubleshooting for individual machines while the Wonderware solution provides a full scale SCADA solution across all machines within a process.

HMI Diagnostics
Add a touchscreen to an existing machine to replace push buttons. At the same time you will be enhancing the machine's run ability and reducing downtime. We can develop custom screens to assist the operator or technician with troubleshooting, display fault codes, safety circuit status, uptime/downtime, parts per minute, predictive maintenance, incorporate Allen-Bradley faceplates for servo drives, frequency drives and I/O.
