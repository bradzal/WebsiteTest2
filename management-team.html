Our Management Team

Over 50 years of combined automation controls experience.

Robert Hattin - President

With over 25 years’ experience in managing and building machinery for the packaging machinery and paper converting industry, Rob’s responsibilities at ProV include business development and strategic planning. He currently serves as Chairman of the Canadian Manufacturer’s and Exporters Association (CME). Rob was past Owner & President of one of Canada’s leading packaging machinery designers and manufacturers from 1998 – 2009

Peter Graham - Engineering Manager

With over 10 years of experience managing, designing and servicing end-of-line packaging machinery and paper converting equipment, Peter’s responsibilities at ProV include coordinating projects and resources for the engineering and service groups. He is a member of the Conestoga College Program Advisory Committee for Robotics and Automation. Peter was a Controls Designer and Engineering Supervisor at of one of Canada’s leading packaging machinery designers and manufacturers 2002 – 2012

Alexander Shalamov – Sr. Applications Specialist

With over 10 years of experience programming, designing and servicing end-of-line packaging machinery and paper converting equipment, Alex’s responsibilities at ProV include research & development and service for key accounts. Alex was a Controls Designer and Lead programmer of one of Canada’s leading packaging machinery designers and manufacturers from 2004 – 2012
