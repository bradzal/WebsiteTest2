Service & Support

image slider by WOWSlider.com v5.5
One of the things that sets ProVantage apart from other automation systems integrators is our commitment to immediate, complete support. Using our extensive knowledge of automation control systems, we can often offer a quick fix to keep your manufacturing line running, then respond quickly and effectively with a long-term solution. We also offer a useful set of services to help you better integrate components into your manufacturing line.

On-Site Support
ProVantage is proud to offer on location support. We offer general support and troubleshooting on site, or over the phone. We can offer a quick fix solution to an immediate problem to keep things up, followed by a thorough design improvement to ensure continuing reliability. We can also quickly offer a firmware upgrade to keep all devices up-to-date and standardize the equipment.

Project Consulting and Concepting
Using our deep experience in the automation industry, ProV offers valuable consulting services to help you meet your project goals. It's always valuable to have an expert on your side who has seen all of the problems facing you before.

Training
Conducted on or off-site, we help you learn the useful information that will help troubleshooting to increase productivity, reduce operation costs, and improve performance of the equipment without adding any extra hardware. We offer a wide array of training topics including PLC, HMI, Networks, VFD and Servo Programming and Troubleshooting.

Energy Audits
Energy is a valuable commodity, and a terrible thing to waste. Using air flow meters to measure air consumption and power monitors to measure 480V/600V/120V current we can establish a baseline consumption factor and provide solutions to reduce the overall power consumption of your devices. These tests provide you with a greater insight into energy consumption across the plant floor, and help you to save money as well as the environment.
