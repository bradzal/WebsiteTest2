Automation Systems Design & Integration

1 2 3
ProVantage Automation's core team has extensive experience designing intelligent automated manufacturing systems. We are industry leaders in integrating PLCs, motion control, HMIs and supervisory control systems. Investing in up-to-date automation technologies is a prudent way to expand your capabilities and save money in the long run.

Controls Design and Integration
We can design and supply entire machine controls packages including electrical, pneumatic, and hydraulic schematics for new installations. We can also design and supply electrical panels.

Motion Control, Programming, and Design
Our automation specialists have commissioned over 1000 axis’ of motion ranging from small 400watt 24VDC servos used for servo adjustment and change-over axis’ to larger 100amp 480V drives used in high speed packaging machines like case packers and lane diverters.

We have extensive experience with designing, programming and servicing simple and complex multi-axis systems using Allen-Bradley Kinetix 6000, Ultra 3000, 1394 on CIP, Sercos, DeviceNet or parallel interface networks.

We also have specialized training and expertise in designing and servicing Schneider Electric brand Servo systems with PacDrive and Elau platforms.

Other servo brands we support are Bosch-Rexroth, Festo, Panasonic and Emerson

PLC Programming
Our automation specialists have programmed and commissioned over 200 packaging systems for case packing, palletizing, diverting and robotic material handling.

We have the software and expertise to support the following PLC manufactures:

  • ● All Allen Bradley brand controllers
  • ● Allen-Bradley Guardlogix Safety Processors
  • ● Schneider Electric, Telemechanique & Modicon
  • ● Siemens S7 & S5
  • ● Panasonic
  • ● Pilz Safety Processors
Legacy Controls Hardware Upgrade
Allow us to redesign your machine controls systems using the latest hardware and software. Eliminate the need to purchase expensive and difficult to find parts and reduce your spare parts inventory. Typical examples of an upgrade are a PLC5 to a Logix 5000 or Compact Logix. Modern controls hardware also opens up the possibility to increase the speed, efficiency and throughput of your manufacturing line.

Safety Circuit Upgrade
Whether you are relocating a machine or simply enhancing the machine's safety integrity, we are capable of designing and installing any category safety system to meet the most stringent applications using hard-wired or safety controller systems.
