Machine & Mechanical Design

ProVantage Automation has experienced mechanical designers on staff that have developed countless custom and specific mechanical assemblies and retrofits to solve problems countless customers have encountered in their processes. We strive to provide our customers with effective, efficient, and safe solutions that are best suited for them and their operations.

Operational Improvements and Retrofits
We can provide sophisticated automation solutions for existing processes, or give your manufacturing line valuable new capabilities. We have strong mechanical engineering design capabilities and years of experience designing complex machines for manufacturing processes. ProVantage is ready to design, install, and support a very wide range of options to help your business grow while maintaining a healthy bottom line.

Machine & Retrofit Design
As a provider of turn-key automation solutions, we offer full mechanical/machine design services. We have deep experience with end-of-line packaging, paper converting and material handling. Here are a few applications we have had a lot of experience implementing:

  • ● Collators
  • ● Stackers
  • ● Packing Systems
  • ● Lane Dividers & Balancers
  • ● Pleaters
  • ● Dynamically Adjustable Cooling Systems
  • ● Robot End of Arm Tooling

Mechanical Solutions Design & Integration
We provide mechanical solutions to a wide-variety of issues or requirements customers need within their plant. We are capable of designing mechanical components, change-parts, and custom solutions to effectively solve the problems you are experiencing in your operations.

Drawing Conversion
Allow us to keep you up-to-date and safe from losing important data by converting your old Micro-fiche or other non electronic designs to AutoCAD.

Project Management
We are committed to successful project implementation, balancing demands on time, cost, performance and people to meet or exceed your needs and expectations. The project manager is the focal point for you and your organization, facilitating the flow of communication and providing regular status reports, project Gantt charts and progress charts.
